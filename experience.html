Experience

My experience comes from working in busy retail environments while studying ICT, leading and supporting teams on shift, and collaborating on university group projects with real industry clients. These roles have taught me to stay calm under pressure, communicate clearly with different people and work confidently with the systems and processes that keep operations running.

Duty Manager – Woolworths Supermarkets

Hobart, TAS · March 2023 – Present

At Woolworths I work as a Duty Manager, responsible for the store team, daily operations and making sure shifts run smoothly. I regularly use POS systems, stock handling tools and staff scheduling systems, and keep basic incident notes so issues are recorded and followed up.

One shift that stands out was around the time of the major CrowdStrike–Microsoft incident in 2024, when a faulty security update caused Windows systems to crash and disrupted many businesses worldwide. In our store this showed up as registers freezing, logins taking longer than usual and some devices needing to be restarted before they could be used. I coordinated with the store manager to move extra staff to the front end, kept customers updated while queues were forming and followed our local restart and fallback procedures until systems stabilised again. Experiencing a global IT outage from the shop floor gave me a practical view of how technical issues at the endpoint level can quickly become business and customer problems. It also reinforced the importance of clear communication, staying calm and having simple workarounds ready—skills that transfer directly into IT support and service desk roles.
